# Outline: Luna in the Fog

**Premise:** Luna gets lost in the fog
**Characters:** Princess Savannah, Luna, Big Al, Bramble the Bat, Ollie the Opossum, The Fireflies
**Setting:** A small village at the edge of a silver lake, surrounded by old woods

## Beats

1. **The peaceful village.** Savannah and Luna live near a silver lake. They do everything together — explore the woods, splash in the shallows, nap in the sun. Luna is loud and bossy and wonderful. She roars at the ducks (the ducks are not impressed). It's a very good life.

2. **The problem.** A thick, soft fog rolls in off the lake one evening — the kind that makes everything quiet and white. By morning it hasn't lifted. Savannah goes outside and calls for Luna, but Luna doesn't come. She can hear Luna somewhere in the fog — roaring, then whimpering, then roaring again — but can't find her. Luna is lost in the fog and getting more upset by the minute.

3. **The call.** Savannah decides to go find her. She visits Big Al, who lives in a stone cottage by the old bridge. He tells her fog like this comes and goes — it won't last forever, but Luna won't know that. "She'll be scared because she can't see you," he says. "Go let her know she's not alone." He gives her a horn carved from wood — "Blow it so she can hear you coming."

4. **The adventure.** Savannah heads into the fog, blowing the horn. She finds Bramble the Bat hanging from a low branch — Bramble can navigate in the dark and offers to scout ahead by sound. They find Ollie the Opossum curled up under a mushroom, playing dead because the fog spooked him. Savannah coaxes him along. The three of them follow Luna's roars deeper into the fog, with Bramble calling directions and Savannah blowing the horn so Luna knows they're coming.

5. **The twist.** They find Luna at the edge of the lake, roaring into the fog — but she's not scared for herself. She found a nest of baby ducks who got separated from their mother in the fog, and she's been standing guard over them, roaring to keep them safe and to call for help. She's been brave the whole time. She was never really lost — she just couldn't leave.

6. **The resolution.** Savannah calls the Fireflies, who rise up from the reeds and light the way across the lake like tiny lanterns. The mother duck follows the light back to her babies. Luna finally relaxes, leans into Savannah, and makes that deep rumbling purr. Ollie pats Luna on the tail. Bramble settles on Luna's head.

7. **Happily ever after.** The fog lifts with the morning sun. They all walk home together along the lakeshore. Luna roars at the ducks one more time — the ducks are still not impressed — and everyone is warm and safe and exactly where they belong.
