# The Lantern Trail — Outline

**Characters:** Princess Savannah, Luna, Bramble the Bat, Thistle the Rabbit, The Fireflies
**Setting:** Forest edge at twilight, a mysterious glowing trail through deep woods, a hidden clearing
**Tone:** Wonder, discovery, invitation. No problem to solve — just beauty to find.

---

## Beats

1. **Warm evening at the forest edge.** Savannah, Luna, Bramble, and Thistle on their usual evening walk. The sky is going gold and purple. Comfortable routine.

2. **The lanterns appear.** Mysterious floating lanterns glow along a forest path none of them have seen before. Curiosity pulls them forward.

3. **The lanterns guide.** Each lantern glows brighter as they approach and dims behind them — always leading forward, deeper into the woods. The path feels safe, not threatening.

4. **Discovery moments along the trail:**
   - Bramble scouts ahead, clicking and listening, reporting back what she hears.
   - Thistle bounces through golden sparks that drift from the lanterns, laughing.
   - Luna breathes gently on each lantern and it hums a different note — together they build a melody as the group walks deeper.
   - Savannah notices tiny wings flickering inside each lantern. Something alive.

5. **The twist / reveal.** The trail ends at a wide, open clearing filled with hundreds of fireflies — the Firefly Gathering, a once-a-year event. The "lanterns" were clusters of fireflies. They lit the trail on purpose, as an invitation, because they've watched Savannah walk the forest edge every evening and wanted her to come.

6. **The resolution.** Nothing to fix. Nothing was wrong. Savannah simply accepts the invitation and is welcomed. The fireflies settle on Luna's black scales, turning her into a glittering constellation. Bramble and Thistle play in the light.

7. **Happily ever after.** They stay until late, then the fireflies light the trail home. One single firefly blinks goodnight at the tree line. And forever after, one small light waits at the tree line each evening, blinking hello.

---

## Notes

- This is NOT "The Lantern Festival" (paper lanterns, lakeside celebration). This is a forest exploration — mysterious glowing trail, deep woods, discovery and invitation.
- No problem to solve. The story is about being noticed, being invited, being welcomed. Pure warmth.
- The fireflies are a collective character — gentle, watchful, generous.
- Luna's constellation moment is the visual climax.
