# Outline: Luna the Dragon

**Based on:** Original fairy tale outline from reference material
**Characters:** Princess Savannah, Luna, Big Al, Wilma, Opossum, Raccoon, Bat

## Beats

1. **The peaceful kingdom.** Princess Savannah lives in a cozy castle at the edge of a great forest. She spends her days with her three best friends — Opossum, Raccoon, and Bat. They play in the meadow, pick wildflowers, and explore the forest paths.

2. **The problem.** Strange rumbling sounds from the mountains. The ground shakes. Smoke rises above the treetops. The kingdom whispers about a fearsome dragon. The animals are nervous. Something is scaring the birds away from the northern woods.

3. **The call.** Savannah isn't scared — she's curious. She decides to find out what's happening. Opossum, Raccoon, and Bat are nervous but won't let her go alone.

4. **Seeking wisdom.** They visit Big Al and Wilma in their cottage at the forest's edge. Big Al — big, strong, burly — knows about dragons. Wilma — elegant, knowing — gives Savannah something for the journey (a lantern, a song, a piece of advice). They trust Savannah to handle this.

5. **The adventure.** The four friends journey toward the mountains. The forest gets darker. The rumbling gets louder. Each critter has a small moment — Opossum freezes and plays dead at a shadow, Raccoon finds a shortcut through a stream, Bat scouts ahead in the dark. They support each other.

6. **The twist.** They find the dragon — Luna. She's big and black-scaled and looks terrifying. But she's not attacking anything. She's crying. The rumbling was her stomach (she's hungry). The smoke was her sneezes (she has a cold). She's lost and alone and scared.

7. **The resolution.** Savannah approaches Luna gently. Offers friendship. The critters bring Luna berries and warm moss. Savannah sings her the song Wilma taught her. Luna calms down. She's not a monster — she's just a dragon who needed a friend.

8. **Happily ever after.** Luna joins the group. They walk back through the forest together — which isn't dark anymore now that they have a dragon to light the way. Big Al and Wilma welcome Luna. The kingdom isn't afraid anymore. Savannah has a new best friend, and Luna is never lonely again.
