# Outline: The Seeds That Sang

**Characters:** Princess Savannah, Luna the Dragon, Digby the Mole, Cora the Crow, Wilma

## Premise

Savannah plants Wilma's mystery seeds that each hum a different note, but they clash and wilt.

## Structure

1. **Peaceful kingdom** — Wilma gives Savannah seven magical seeds, each one humming a different note. Savannah plants them in a row in Wilma's garden.
2. **The problem** — The seeds sprout into humming flowers, but the notes clash and the melody is all wrong. The flowers begin to droop and wilt from the discord.
3. **The call** — Savannah decides to rearrange the flowers to find the right melody. She gathers Luna, Digby, and Cora to help.
4. **The adventure** — Repeated pattern: dig, listen, move, try again. Each round the melody gets closer.
   - Digby listens underground to feel the roots' vibrations
   - Cora listens from above to hear the pattern from the sky
   - Luna digs (with loud opinions about where things should go)
   - Savannah decides the final arrangement each round
5. **The twist** — A hidden 8th seed (a silent white flower) has been buried beneath the others all along. It is the "rest note" — the pause in the melody that makes the song complete. Wilma appears and explains.
6. **The resolution** — With the rest note in place, the seven humming flowers and one silent bloom create a perfect lullaby together.
7. **Happily ever after** — The singing garden blooms every evening, humming lullabies as the sun goes down.
